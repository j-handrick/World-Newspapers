Hello!

This is a website designed and developed as an unsolicited redesign to World-Newspapers.com. The website is built on a great idea, but was lacking what some might call 'style' or 'functionality'. We gave it a facelift, and added a bit of functionality to it by contributing some RSS feeds to the topics, so that people may be more inclined to actually read news from other places around the world.
